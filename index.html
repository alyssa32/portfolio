Alyssa Durdey

Computational Artist & Designer

My artistic practice explores the complex relationship between nature, technology, and human influence. Through a combination of 3D modeling, drawing, and mixed media, I look at how perception shapes our understanding of the environment and challenge the ways we interact with the natural world. My work often reflects on how nature is framed, controlled, or altered, inviting viewers to reconsider what is seen. My work serves as a bridge between reality and perception, encouraging a deeper awareness of the environment and our place within it.
